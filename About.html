About Us

Welcome to Your Food Sharing Website, where our mission is to reduce food waste and help those in need by connecting food donors with organizations and individuals in search of excess food.

Our platform provides a simple and efficient way for donors to list surplus food items from events, parties, or businesses, and for organizations to discover and collect these donations to distribute to people who can benefit from them.

We believe that by facilitating the sharing of excess food, we can make a positive impact on both the environment and the lives of those facing food insecurity. Together, we can make a difference.

If you have any questions or feedback, please feel free to contact us. We appreciate your support in our mission to create a more sustainable and compassionate world.
